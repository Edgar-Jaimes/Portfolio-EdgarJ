Horse Plinko Cyber Challenge (HPCC)

Project Image

Project Description

The Horse Plinko Cyber Challenge (HPCC) is a competitive cybersecurity event designed to simulate real-world defensive security operations. I participated as a member of the Blue Team, where the primary objective was to protect Windows and Linux servers from ongoing, live attacks launched by a Red Team.

During the competition, my team was responsible for maintaining system availability while actively monitoring for suspicious activity and responding to security incidents in real time. This required balancing multiple priorities at once, such as identifying threats, applying defensive changes, and ensuring that critical services remained operational despite constant attack attempts.

I actively contributed to system defense by monitoring network traffic and system behavior, investigating potential compromises, and helping implement mitigation strategies. The competition environment closely mirrored real incident-response scenarios, where quick decision-making, communication, and technical awareness are essential to minimizing downtime and impact.

Throughout the event, my team successfully coordinated defensive efforts under pressure and maintained over 80% server uptime, demonstrating effective incident response and teamwork in a high-stress environment.

Skills Learned

  • Hands-on experience with Blue Team defensive operations
  • Incident response fundamentals, including triage and prioritization
  • Monitoring and defending Windows and Linux servers
  • Network traffic analysis using tools such as Wireshark
  • Use of security tools including RDP and Kali Linux
  • Team communication and coordination under time pressure

Outcome

This competition strengthened my understanding of defensive cybersecurity concepts and reinforced the importance of teamwork, communication, and adaptability during active security incidents. The experience provided a realistic look into Blue Team responsibilities and helped me build confidence in responding to threats while maintaining system availability.
